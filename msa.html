Venus

Your lover

Yet your second best

Your sun

But not the light

But don't you worry

I'm used to it

Ever since I was a child

to them times when I couldn't have anything in mind

but I still knew

wasn't new

The child that grew up

as a dull spark

always left there alone in the beautiful park

when they could take me with them

was still left there alone

but my eyes never shed tears

my heart wasn't broken into million pieces

do you wonder when all that happend

when my heart burnt and the eyes with tears filled

it was you the whole reason

yes

oh yes to be the girl

the only that you would always think about

write about

sing about

cry about

but i know

but i know

oh boy do I know

though this is all

your the one that my heart always call

because you made me feel like the sun,the moon and all

yeah you told me you picked me out of all them stars

and even defined me as the moon of your world

but little did I know I was your Venus.
